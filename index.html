About ITS

The Information Technology Services department (ITS) supports the College's IT services such as e-mail, telephone, internet access, Wi-Fi, website maintenance, Banner, Access Rio, SARS-Grid, tech training, etc.

We are dedicated to providing quality and timely support to the campus community.

If you need assistance with your account, network access, telephone system, moving your computer, or anything IT related please contact us through the help desk
