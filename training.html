Banner Navigation Training

Banner Navigation Training is available upon request. Please contact Human Resources and obtain and complete a Banner security form prior to scheduling training. Banner Navigation Training usually takes 1hr 30min to 2hr to complete the training and the testing.

You will receive your account shortly after successfully completing Banner Navigation Training. The security form will be submitted to the appropriate supervisor who assigns access privileges and then our support team will activate the account and notify you via e-mail.

Virtual Office Hours

IT Training will be holding virtual office hours throughout the semester using CCCConfer web-conferencing.

The idea behind virtual office hours is that we can have small groups and/or individuals pop-in for a specific question such as "how can I make my powerpoint file size smaller?" or "How do I archive my Outlook PST file?". Using CCCConfer for the virtual office hours allows part-time and off-campus staff members an easy way to utilize our training resources.
