Change Your SARS-Grid Password

You can change your SARS-Grid password at any time. You may change this when you are assigned a new account or periodically. We suggest changing your SARS-Grid password each time you change your Rio Hondo network password. They are two separate passwords.

Open SARS-Grid

Open SARS-Grid in the usual fashion (you may have a desktop icon or prefer the Start menu)

At the SARS-Grid login window, type the word "change" in the password field instead of your regular password.

Password:change

Hit the enter key or click Continue.

You will see your username in the Password Control box. Enter your current password (the one you want to change *from*) and enter the new password (the one you want to change *to*) in both the New Password and Confirm New Password fields.

Current Password: *old* password

New Password: *desired* password

Confirm New Password: *desired* password again


Tap the enter key or click Continue to change your password.
