Create a SARS-Grid Guest ID

Sometimes students will schedule appointments that do not yet have a student ID. We can create a Guest ID in SARS-GRID in order to serve them until they do obtain a student ID.

From the Student Appointment screen, press F9 on your keyboard to switch to the Guest ID mode.

Press F7 on your keyboard to generate a new Guest ID.

Please enter there name, birthdate, and any contact information at this time.

Names associated with Guest ID’s will appear normally when adding appointments.
