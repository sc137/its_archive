How to Configure an iOS Device

Your Rio Hondo email works with your iOS device. You can setup your iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch to synchronize your email, calendar, contacts, and to-do items.

Please follow the steps below to configure your Rio Hondo exchange account on your iOS device. Click on the images to enlarge them.


Please ensure that your device have a good data connection or that your device is connected to wi-fi.

Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad and touch Mail, Contacts, Calendars.

Touch Add Account…

Touch Microsoft Exchange

Enter your full email address for Rio Hondo in the Email field and include the @riohondo.edu.

Enter riohondo for the Domain and enter your network username and password. This is the same way you login to your computer or to access rio. Touch Next.

You may change the Description field to something more descriptive such as "Rio Hondo" or "Work Email".

Confirm your information is correct and then touch Next.

Select the services that you want to sync with your device.

Please contact the help desk if you need further assistance.
