Audio/Visual Support

E-mail: avsupport@riohondo.edu
Phone: (562) 463-3752

We provide Audio / Visual support for meetings and events on campus. We have a Public Address system, a couple of projector's, and a couple of laptops available.

Please include the following information in your A/V support request:

  • Date of Event or Meeting
  • Room Number
  • Start & End Time
  • Equipment Needed

Please use the email link above to submit a request. The email will be sent to both the help desk and A/V Support.
